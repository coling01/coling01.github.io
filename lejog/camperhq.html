CamperHQ

Role of the camperHQ

The camper is HQ and provides rider support, logistics, food, hygiene and maybe some sleep.

It is a Lugano camper ( same as sleeper ) hired from Freedhome with spec listed here ... Lugano Spec

We decided to drop the additional support van to give more flexibilty since we are self supporting and less drivers will give us more flexibilty when moving riders between groups.

  • The CamperHQ will have a dedicated mobile phone so that the riders or sleeper can contact if required.
  • A Barclays debit card on the JogLe acount for buying fuel or provisions with obvious PIN
  • Cook food ( see food page ) for riders coming off shift ( assuming shift of 2 hours+ )
  • Serve food for off-riders who have come off shift and socialise ( 20-30 mins )
  • Toilet and wash facilities. Quick showers at a push.
  • Drive up the road after feeding off-riders
  • Liase with sleeper to know where to meet up for rider changeover.
  • Laptop with maps and navigation to pinpoint sleeper, van and possibly riders.
  • Charging points for lights, phones, garmins
  • Drying facility if wet weather ( somehow )
  • Some beds for those who happy to sleep on move ( 1/3 of the time ) or parked ( 1/3 of the time )
  • Carry 4 bikes on rack when required
  • Possibly carry 2 or 3 bikes in bunk bed area if required, such as long drive up / down

Role of Logistics Support on board camper

  • Delegate all activities below to a next-support coming on shift as required
  • Liase with next-riders coming on shift to agree distance to be covered ( related to shift duration of course ) and pin point the next meet point from Matts list of meet points.
  • Communicate meet-point with riders prior to getting on bikes and with next shift Sleeper driver
  • Track Sleeper and lead CamperHQ to meet-point
  • Delegate any roles to rider from each team when necessary
  • Help cook and serve food to riders coming off shift
  • Ensure all vehicles know when to refuel and instruct drivers accordingly
  • Be familiar with route for navigation assistance if required by sleeper or van
  • Assist riders coming off to get lights on charge in CamperHQ
  • Setup food area outside camperHQ if weather OK else eating area inside camper if not
  • Clear up after eating
  • Help offload new riders bikes at meet-point before incoming riders arrive
  • Help load up old riders bikes

Role of the riders on board camper

  • Help support / pickup any roles not covered by support or delegated by support
  • Drive the camper ( likely required ) 1/3 of the time
