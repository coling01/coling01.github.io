Sleeper

Role of the Sleeper

The sleeper is a 6 berth camper van with the primary function of providing sleep.

It is a Lugano camper ( same as HQ Camper ) hired from Freedhome. The spec for the Lugano is here Lugano

  • The sleeper will have a dedicated mobile phone so that the camper or van can contact if required.
  • The sleeper be used to head up the road as quickly as possible to provide opportunities to sleep.
  • Carry 4 bikes on rack as required
  • It will have some food supplies but most catering will be done in the HQ
  • ... 2 full size dedicated bunk beds at the rear
  • ... A drop down double bed that takes about 20 seconds to deploy
  • ... A single bed on the drivers side of the dining area which requires no setup
  • ... A 3/4 double bed on the passenger side of the dining area which is very complicated probably takes 5 minutes to setup.
  • It would make sense to leave the dining area set up for sleeping, but this may or may not leave enough seats for travelling
