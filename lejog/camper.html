Camper

Role of the camper

The camper is HQ and provides logistics, food, hygiene and maybe some sleep.
  • The camper will have a dedicated mobile phone so that the van or car can contact if required.
  • Cook food ( see food page ) for riders coming off shift ( assuming shift of 2 hours+ )
  • Serve food for off-riders who have come off shift and socialise ( 20-30 mins )
  • Toilet and wash facilities. Quick showers at a push.
  • Drive up the road after feeding off-riders
  • Liase with car and van to know where to meet up for rider changeover.
  • Hopefully laptop with maps and navigation to pinpoint car, van and possibly riders.
  • Charging points for lights, phones, garmins
  • Drying facility if wet weather ( somehow )
  • Some beds for those who happy to sleep on move ( 1/3 of the time ) or parked ( 1/3 of the time )
  • Transport for all 8 none riders bikes. No spare bike needed, all riders will buddy with a rider of similar size in another team to share bike in the event of bike problems. Need easy segregation of bikes to allow east identification. Maybe 4 on roof and 4 on rack / trailer. Or bikes labelled up with colour coded labels.

Role of support on board camper

In order of priority if we dont have much support ( which is highly likely and would mean more tasks falling to riders in camper ! )
  • Delegate all activities below to a next-rider coming on shift if required
  • Liase with next-riders coming on shift to agree distance to be covered ( related to shift length of course which is agreed with all riders the forthcoming shift lengths)
  • Give written instructions to riders coming off shift as the approximate location of the changeover
  • Track car and lead camper to car and van leading rider meetup for rider changeover
  • Delegate these roles to rider from each team when necessary
  • Help cook and serve food to riders coming off shift
  • Ensure all vehicles know when to refuel and instruct drivers accordingly
  • Be familiar with route for navigation assistance if required by car or van
  • Assist riders coming off to get lights on charge
  • Setup food area outside van if weather OK else eating area inside van if not
  • Clear up after eating
  • Help offload new riders bikes at meeting point before incoming riders arrive
  • Help load up old riders bikes

Role of the riders on board camper

  • Help support / pickup any roles not covered by support or delegated by support
  • Drive the camper ( likely required ) 1/3 of the time
