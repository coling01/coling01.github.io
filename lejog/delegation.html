  • Identify potential stopping / rendevous points or other useful locations such as bike shops, places we can get a brew, general shops and the nearest A&E.
  • Identify some options for refill / empty water tanks ( relatively easy, suggest that we can simply empty grey tank in road drains ! )
  • Identify some options for empty chemical toilet. May need campsite for this.
  • Provide strava routes in suitable block sizes, maybe 100 miles or so

Route Checking Goals

  • To carefully go through Matts route and confirm its as good as can be
  • Compare with sustrans routes map Map and ensure the strava route lines up when it should
  • Check that any a-roads are not dual carriageways
  • Try to confirm that minor roads are surfaced and not off road dirt tracks
  • Confirm that avoiding any major towns or cities that would lead to delays at traffic lights or junctions.
  • Check that got optimum height avoidance where possible
  • Check that distance is good at 1000+ miles

Nav Goals

We have a group of riders who should normally be together ( excepting climbs ) and support van, camperHQ an sleeper. We require

  • Riders to be able to follow a route on garmin or phone ( say Strava ) using a suitable device mount on handlebars
  • At least one rider in group, preferably all, to beacon their position so they be tracked by Van and CamperHQ
  • Each vehicle to beacon their position to allow visibility by the other vehicles.
  • Each vehicle to have navigation visibility of each other and riders. Presumably on a mac or laptop
  • We require 3 mobile phones to allow vehicles to always reliably contact each, also for riders to know what number to ring for support
  • Mission is to find the best app to use for phones to beacon and laptops or macs in vehicles.
  • And liase with rest of team to source necessary hardware / ensure everybody has necessary kit.
  • And sims for phones and laptops for internet access
  • But not worry about the route itself, Matt is handling that. Maybe liase with Matt to ensure the route is available on selected apps
