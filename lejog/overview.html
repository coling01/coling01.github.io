Overview

Plan is to ride from LandsEnd to JohnOGroats over 3 days of Easter weekend in a continuous relay. 3 groups of riders, each group between 2 and 5 riders, grouped by speed.

Approximate timings

  • Thursday 13/4 evening - Travel to start point
  • Friday 14/4- Ride 24*7
  • Saturday 15/4 - Ride 24*7
  • Sunday 16/4 - Ride 24*7
  • Monday 17/4 - Return from end point

Assumptions

  • 12 riders and 2 support.
  • 3 groups of riders with 3 to 5 riders per group.
  • Begin LeJog 01:00 Friday morning
  • Arrive LeJog 07:00 Monday morning
  • 78 hours riding gives minimum target speed of 13mph.
  • 13mph average is easily achievable across 3 groups average 15/13/11 mph.

Vehicles

  • Bikes - 4 riders on road
  • Camper - General HQ providing food hygiene and logistics. Transport 4 riders plus support.
  • Van - Close support for riders continually leap frogging the riders +-5 miles. Transport 2 riders.
  • Car - Heads up the road for sleeping. Transport 2 riders.
