Overview

d days / w weeks till LeJog Easter 2017

This page gives brief details of the trip. More details in the relevant pages.

Plan is to ride from LandsEnd to JohnOGroats over 3 days of Easter weekend in a continuous relay. 3 groups of riders, each group between 2 and 5 riders, grouped by speed.

  • Thurs 13th April Approx 16:00 Travel to Start
  • Good Friday 14th April Riding
  • Sat 15th April Riding
  • Easter Sunday 16th April Riding
  • Easter Monday 17th April Arrive Destination early morning. Travel back.

Assumptions

  • 12 riders. Still hoping for some support, maybe just for half the trip.
  • 3 groups of riders with 3 to 5 riders per group.
  • Groups are referred to as riders ( active ), off-riders ( just come off shift this not back on bikes for 2 shifts ), next-riders ( next up )
  • Begin LeJog 01:00 Friday morning. Arrive LeJog 09:00 Monday morning which gives us 80 hours to cover the distance.
  • 1000 miles over 80 hours means an average speed of 12mph. But likely we can go quicker and have some complete breaks. ( See speed section for breakdowns )
  • The shift duration needs discussion and agreement but expected to be between 1 hour and 4 hours. Maybe we have shorter shifts during the day to allow more socialising and longer shifts during the night to allow more sleep.

Vehicles

  • Bikes - 4 riders on road
  • HQ Camper - General HQ providing food hygiene and logistics. Transport 4 riders plus support. CamperHQ details here
  • Van - Close support for riders continually leap frogging the riders +-5 miles. Transport 2 riders. Sleeper details here
  • Sleeper - Camper that heads up the road for sleeping. Transport 2 riders. Van details here
