Rota

The proposed schedule is along the lines of

  • After riders change shift, all 8 off duty riders will stop together and eat / socialise for 20 to 30 minutes
  • The riders coming off shoft are off duty. The off duty sleeper-driver will drive the sleeper up the road to the next meeting point. Other off duty riders are free to head up the road with the sleeper-driver to sleep in sleeper, ride with support van or socialise in CamperHQ. The sleeper will head straight to the next meeting point.
  • The van driver and assistant ( who is also sleeper-driver ) will chase down the riders and support them for the rest of the shift. The assistant may drive the Van if required
  • The logistics and CamperHQ-Driver will clear up after mealtime and head up the road to the next meeting point
  • ... some time will pass with CamperHQ and Sleeper parked up at rendevous point
  • The logistics and CamperHQ-Driver will prepare food for the riders coming off shift
  • The Van driver and assistant will leave the riders approximately 15 minutes from the meeting point to get ready to ride.
  • The riders will arrive at the meeting point
  • The new riders will head straight off, the last riders will eat / socialise for 20 to 30 minutes
  • ...repeat to Lands End

Time Break Down At Any Point

1 - There 4 riders on the road

2 - There are 4 riders just come off shift who are now off-duty.

  • They will eat briefly
  • 1 will drive the sleeper which will take approx 1/3 of the shift time, assuming camper will travel 3 times quicker than riders.
  • 3 will be completely off duty

3 - There are 4 riders about to come on shift who are now on support duty

  • CamperHQ driver and logistics in HQ
  • Van driver and assistant in support Van ( note that Van assistant is also Sleeper driver )

Time break down per role per 24 hour period

Each rider will spend 1/3 of the time on the bike. This equates to approximately 8 hours per day

Off Duty Time

Whilst off duty, each rider can decide how to spend their time

  • Any problems or issues will initially be dealt with by the support van and CamperHQ but of course anybody maybe needed to step in
  • They may choose to head up the road in the sleeper campervan
  • They may choose to socialise / help with CamperHQ functions
  • They may choose to ride with support van or even drive the support van if driver is tired
  • They may choose to ride with the current group
