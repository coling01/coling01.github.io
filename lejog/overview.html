Overview

d days / w weeks till JogLe Easter 2017

This page gives brief details of the trip. More details in the relevant pages.

Plan is to ride from JohnOGroats to LandsEnd over 3 days of Easter weekend in a continuous relay. 3 groups of riders, each group between 3 and 5 riders, grouped by speed.

  • Thurs 13th April Approx 16:00 Travel to Start
  • Good Friday 14th April Riding
  • Sat 15th April Riding
  • Easter Sunday 16th April Riding
  • Easter Monday 17th April Arrive Destination early morning. Early evening travel back up North.

Assumptions

  • 12 riders which is the maximum we can transport in 2 Campers.
  • 3 groups of riders with 3 to 5 riders per group.
  • Rotations will be a) Ride b) Rest c) Support ... repeat.
  • Begin Jog 01:00 Friday morning. Arrive Le 09:00 Monday morning which gives us 80 hours to cover the distance.
  • 1000 miles over 80 hours means an average speed of 12mph. But possible we may go quicker and have some complete breaks. ( See speed section for breakdowns )
  • The shift duration needs discussion and agreement but expected to be between 1 hour and 4 hours. Maybe we have shorter shifts during the day to allow more socialising and longer shifts during the night to allow more sleep.
  • Still a little worried about the wind so hedging in case we need to switch direction. Tracking flight prices here. travel
