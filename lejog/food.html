Food

Camper Food

  • The food will be arranged / prepped by the logistics role.
  • Primary aim of providing plenty of carbs with some protein
  • Secondary aim of of being very simple to prepare, nobody wants to spend long in kitchen
  • Ensure copious amounts are available so anybody can eat hot food whenever the camper does a food stop, after rider changeover
  • Pasta, rice, noodles, potatoes, beans lentils
  • Simple sauces with chicken/mince/ham/tuna/mushroom/salami/cheese tomato creamy curry sauce
  • Both campers have ovens so maybe pizza
  • Plenty of bread
  • Hot drinks such as tea, coffee, hot chocolate
  • Cakes and biscuits
  • Fruit
  • Cereals, bread / toast / jam, porridge
  • Milk, Orange juice
  • Crisps, nuts
  • Any special dietary requirements will be taken into account, of course
  • Any suggestions or ideas ?
  • Will make some simple recipes and stock the camper accordingly
  • Some quick dried pasta / noodle / rice dishes that can be made with boiling water for emergency food / available in the sleeper.
  • Some tins of big soup that can be quickly prepared if anybody wants a quick snack with some bread.

Bought in Food

  • Riders in sleeper, van or CamperHQ may occasionally stop into a town when passing near or through to get something they might fancy such as chippy or pizza etc.
  • This option will be known before the riders set off as the rendezvous will be decided and chosen from Matts 10 mile nav stops.
  • If riders do want bought in food, this will be ordered / bought by the CamperHQ and logistics as the riders arrive at rendezvous.
