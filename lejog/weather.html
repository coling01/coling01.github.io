Weather

We need to plan for poor weather. This may mean considering the following, nearer the time.
  • More spare gear for riders and having gear handy in van
  • Having some capability for drying riders clothers in camper
  • Possible impact on rider navigation with wet devices / poor batteries
  • Sun would be nice and good chance in April we would just need to ensure sun cream available in van and plenty of fluids
  • Wind could be a big issue if we have a strong headwind and will be addressed here
  • If the weather is particularly cold and we get snow or ice then that could be a big issue. Not sure what we would do in this case.
