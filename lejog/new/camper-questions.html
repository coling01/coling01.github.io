Camper Questions

After seeing the campers we decided to go for 2 Lugano.

The main reason for this are

  • The Marco Polo was high profile because of over cab bed, which we did not really need in the CamperHQ. Better to have 2 bunk beds more comfortable for 2 blokes
  • The Lugano has dedicate bunk beds at the bank thus more convenient for a quick nap.
  • Getting both campers the same means less handover instructions, saving time for the named drivers when we pickup.
  • The lower profile of the Lugano will be easier drive and less fuel
